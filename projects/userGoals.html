User Goals

Vagabonding


Question: How will your users understand the content of the project?
They will know that the content is just other travelers that can be contacted.

Question: How will your users understand the primary objectives?
The users will know that the app really serves as a means to see the people around you.

Question: How do your users think your interactive projects should work and what are their experience levels with the type of media you are creating?
They will know how it works because I think they will be very used to this type of app. It can be almost tinder-like. But no swiping left or right--you can just see everyone and decide who to talk to.
