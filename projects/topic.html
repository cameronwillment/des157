Topic Choice

The topic I have chosen is to create an app that allows users to rent items with their neighbours. Users can upload a picture of their lawnmower, for example and other users can rent that product from them. Other apps have tried this, but I think the key with mine is that it allows people to form groups where only other people in their group can see their add. This fostors a sense of community and gets rid of concerns about letting the public know what things you have.
