Storyboards

I tried out two ideas in class for the storyboard. The first was an app that allows
people to rent or borrow items from people near them. I used an example of a person
traveling in Paris who need to get to a place accross town. Taxis and Uber are expensive, if only he had a bike.
So he opens the app, sees someone near him offering to rent out their bike--so he rents it from them!
He then uses it to go to the place and everything works great.

My second storyoard is a similar situation--a traveler in Paris--but who is looking to get to
know people and to get a meal. So he checks his app and posts on it that he is a hungry
traveler looking to eat some dinner and meet people. He gets an alert that someone has invited him to dinner!
The next scene he is sitting with a local family eating dinner and absorbing their culture.

I have decided to go with a different-somewhat similar--idea for my project. I thought the market
for these ideas were too small--not many people
want to rent their stuff out and even fewer are willing to invite a stranger to their home.
But after doing this and talking to people I came up with the idea to have an app that showed other
travelers in the area. Kinda like a tinder for travelers trying to meet up. You can open it and see other travelers
within x-miles from you and message them to see if they wanna hang out! (I call it Vagabonding)
